KM 9,5 de la Ruta 52, Canning, Provincia de Buenos Aires, Argentina

Developed on an area of 264 hectares, with more than 65% destined to common green spaces, La Providencia is a unique Resort & Country Club located in the Canning-San Vicente corridor, at Km 9.5 of Route 52 in the town of Canning, Province of Buenos Aires.

It has an unbeatable access, located only 35 minutes from downtown Buenos Aires.

You will be able to live in one of its 715 lots, located in its four sectors: Polo, Golf, Village and Lakes in a framework of maximum security with the highest and most exclusive technology in the area.

How to get there?

Calle Libertad 310, Escobar, Pcia. de Buenos Aires, Argentina

Located in the town of Maschwitz, district of Escobar (Km. 45 of Panamericana).

Very good location and excellent access.

Developed on an area of 500 hectares.

Composed of 4 neighborhoods of single-family houses: North, Islands, Golf and Port (communicated among them by a fast road).

Average area of the lots 800m2.

65% of the lots overlook internal lagoons and in the Golf neighborhood 50% of the lots overlook the golf course.

Two sports sectors (Golf Club and Sports Club).

Golf course, 3 tennis courts, volleyball court, soccer field, swimming pool, playground for children.

There are also two sectors for future medium densities. One in the Golf area (sup 16,019 m2) and another near the sports area in the highest area of El Canton overlooking the central lagoon (sup 40,076 m2).

On the plots bordering El Canton, an important residential and commercial area is planned that will have all the necessary services to be able to live and work in the same place: Supermarket, Shopping Center, Free Standing Shops, Home Center, Gas Station and a sector planned for a future train station. It will also have a marina with an exit to the Lujan River through a 4 km channel.
